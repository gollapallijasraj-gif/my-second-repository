How to beat Minecraft

step 1:-

get wood and make crafting table & sticks

step 2:-

make a wooden pickaxe in crafting table

step 3:-

mine stone with wooden pickaxe

step 4:-

make a stone pickaxe in crafting table

step 5-

mine raw iron and coal with stone pickaxe

step 6-

make a furnace with stone with crafting table

step 7-

smelt raw iron in furnace with coal to get iron ingots

step 8-

craft iron armour ,bucket , sword, and pickaxe in crafting table

step 9-

mine diamonds with iron pickaxe

step 10:-

make diamond pickaxe, sword in crafting table

step 11:-

mine obsidian from lava mixed with water by putting them down using bucket

step 12:-

make a 4 by 5 nether portal from obsidian and light it using flint and steel(steel means iron ingot) and flint from mining gravel

step 13:-

before you go into the portal craft a shield which would be effective against blazes its crafted with 1 iron ingot and 6 planks

step 13:-

enter nether portal and find a nether fortress and kill blazes (floating heads with rods spinning around them) to get blaze rods

step 14:-

get ender pearls from killing enderman (skinny black mob not be racist) and craft blaze powder from blaze rods to craft eyes of ender 16

step 15:-

go to get to the strong hold throw an eye of ender and follow it

step 16;-

locate the end portal (its like the nether portal but its 3 by 3 horizontally) and place the eyes of ender on it to activate it

step 17:-

before you go to the end get a water bucket and then enter the end

step 18:-

dig out or bridge to the island where the ender dragon is and tower upto the end crystals and place 2 block between you and crystal and then punch it and to get down place the water bucket to safely get to the ground

step 19:-

do the same with all crystals and then hit the ender dragon when it perches until its dead and watch out cuz it'll shoot fireballs which hurt a lot.

step 20:-

if you want you can collect the dragon egg by punching it so that it will teleport and then break a block under another block and place a torch which is crafted using stick and coal and then break the block under the egg

step 21:-

go through the bedrock portal to receive the end credits and then you will be teleported back to the overworld and congrats! you have beaten the game

BEWARE there are hostile mobs that will want to attack you during night defend yourself and eat food from killing sheep ,pigs ,and cows
