How to make a webpage

step 1:-

open notepad

step 2:-

type <!DOCTYPE html> & press enter

step 3:-

type <html> & press enter

step 4:-

type <head> & press enter

step 5:-

type <title>________</title> in place of _ type your webpage name & press enter

step 6:-

type </head> & press enter

step 7:-

type <tbody>______________</body> & in place of _ type what will be te content of your webpage & press enter

step 8:-

type </html>

step 9:-

press ctrl+s & your webpage name.html

step 10:-

locate your finished project and double click it to show it
